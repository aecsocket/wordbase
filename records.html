見るみる
  • 1-dantransitive
      • to see
      • to look
      • to watch
      • to view
      • to observe
      １０ページの５行目をみなさい。
      Let's begin with the fifth line on page 10.[1]
      • to examine
      • to look over
      • to assess
      • to check
      • to judge
      頭あたまがくらくらしているので、深ふかく考かんがえずにいい加か減げんなことを書かいてみる。
      My head is swimming, so I'll try writing any old stuff without any deep thought.[2]
      • to look after
      • to attend to
      • to take care of
      • to keep an eye on
      私わたしも親おやの面めん倒どうとか見みられないですね。
      I can't look after my parents and such either.[3]
      See also看みる
      to look after (often medically); to take care of
      • to experience
      • to meet with (misfortune, success, etc.)
      映えい画がを観みに行いかない？
      Would you like to take in a movie?[4]
  • aux-verb1-dankana
      • to try ...
      • to have a go at ...
      • to give ... a try
      Note
      after the -te form of a verb
      私わたしたちはお互たがいを理り解かいしてみるべきだ。
      We should try to understand one another.[5]
      • to see (that) ...
      • to find (that) ...
      Note
      as 〜てみると, 〜てみたら, 〜てみれば, etc.
  • forms
    • 見る
    • 観る
    • 視る
